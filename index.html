Welcome to my site!

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer with experience in Ruby on Rails and Coldfusion. I've worked solo as well as on a team and in both cases, my love for coding drives me to produce the best of the best of the best, Sir! With honors.

This is actually a new field for me. I graduated with a degree in Biochemistry and a minor in Biology and while there was one programming course that was part of my curriculum, it wasn't very intersting to me. Programming didn't become a part of my life until after about 10 years of working in biology labs facilitating research.

Some how, I don't actually remember why I decided to, I enrolled in a Java course. It turned out that it wasn't as difficult as I expected and eventually I moved into Web Dev.

Long story short, I fell in love with coding and started spending most of my free time learning web technologies through coding and reading. Slowly I built upon what I was learning and found myself in the job market. And now I do what I love for a living. Its a new feeling, and I'm grateful to have it.
